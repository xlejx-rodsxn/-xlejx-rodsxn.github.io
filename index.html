Welcome to my academic profile. I post here my most recent publications and current work, as well as discussions of academic papers in computational social sciences (CSS). My current affiliation is at University of Potsdam, where I study innovative methods to quantify irregular migration.

In my research, I focus on the use of machine learning (ML) methods to inform social science research, often combining multiple data sources, as well as traditional longitudinal and cross-sectional surveys. I am interested in both prediction and causal inference type of questions, as well as their fruitful combinations to explore, describe, and answer causal queries in social science research.
